Some of the most popular E-sport games:

Starcraft 2, a real time strategy game, possibly the oldest E-sport title in the world
Rocket League, a unique game combining driving and football in an interesting and demanding way
Counter Strike: Global Offensive, a team based shooter that took part in making E-sports so popular
Overwatch, a team based hero shooter, another game that's easy to learn but hard to master
Rainbow Six Siege, an objective based shooter with an impressive environment destruction model
League of Legends, a Multiplayer Online Battle Arena game, possibly the biggest E-sport at the moment

Some of the most popular Singleplayer games:

The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim, a role-playing game released on 7 different platforms over the years
The Witcher 3, a single player role-playing game, often considered the best game ever made
Stardew Valley, a very popular casual game about farming and exploration
Grand Theft Auto V, one of the entries in the popular series of games about gangsters
God of War, an action adventure game that proved that single player games are still popular
Red Dead Redemption 2, a role-playing game with a vast and detailed world that's still not fully explored

Some of the most popular Multiplayer games:

Destiny 2, an online only multiplayer looter shooter with elements of PvE and PvP
Left4Dead 2, an old but still updated and played co-op zombie shooter
Call of Duty Modern Warfare, the newest installment in a popular series of fast-paced multiplayer shooters
World of Tanks, one of many free-to-play games with a tree-based progression system
World of Warcraft, one of the most popular Massively Multiplayer Online games of all time
ArmA 3, a hardcore shooter with roleplaying elements, a "war simulator"
